## 🐛 Troubleshooting

### "I lost my data / Logged out"
- Your data is saved in browser LocalStorage
- Clear browser data will delete everything
- Browsers may clear LocalStorage if not used for 30+ days
- Screenshot important data as backup

### "Quiz won't unlock"
- Complete the lesson first (click "Mark as Complete")
- Refresh the page
- Check that you've viewed all sections

### "Can't see forum posts"
- Refresh the page
- Try a different browser
- Check if posts were actually submitted

### "Page looks strange"
- Try different browser
- Clear browser cache
- Zoom to 100%
- Try mobile or desktop mode

### "Demo doesn't load"
- Refresh page
- Clear browser cache
- Try in private/incognito window

## 🌟 Features Explained

### Quiz System
- **How it works:** Read question → Choose answer → Get feedback
- **Passing:** 70% (3.5/5 correct)
- **Multiple attempts:** Retake after passing to improve
- **What's tested:** Content from that day's lesson

### Assignment System
- **Text submissions:** Copy/paste from document or type directly
- **Editing:** Update your work anytime before deadline
- **Rubrics:** Shows what's evaluated
- **Due dates:** One per day, all by Day 5

### Forum System
- **No moderation:** Post freely (keep respectful)
- **Anonymity:** Shows your name, not hidden
- **Replies:** Click "Reply" to respond to posts
- **Deletions:** Only you can delete your posts

### Daily Challenge
- **New each day:** Random environmental task
- **Quick wins:** 10 points for completion
- **Examples:** Clean space, reduce waste, plant something
- **Real impact:** Actually do the challenge!

## 💬 Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I take lessons in any order?**
A: No, they're sequential. Day 2 unlocks after Day 1. This ensures proper knowledge building.

**Q: Do I have to complete everything in one day?**
A: No! You can spread it over weeks. Take your time.

**Q: What if I fail a quiz?**
A: Retake it! Review the lesson first for better understanding.

**Q: Can I delete my progress?**
A: Clearing browser cache/storage will delete everything. Be careful!

**Q: Is my data saved if I close the browser?**
A: Yes! LocalStorage persists across sessions. You won't lose data.

**Q: Can I get extra credit?**
A: Yes! Every activity (forum, challenges, etc.) earns points.

**Q: Is this course graded?**
A: Not officially, but you can track your own progress and points.
